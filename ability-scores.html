Ability Scores, Basic Stats and Proficiencies

Your ability scores (the raw number, i.e. 14) and ability modifiers (the bonus, i.e. the +2) are fairly straightforward. I made no changes here.

Your proficiency score is a flat bonus that you apply in a number of places. Your proficiency bonus goes up by one every fourth level - race and class don't matter.

So, beginning at level 5, this bonus will be +3 for everyone

Your proficiency bonus is added to the skills you're proficient in. You get skill proficiencies from 3 places (see p. 15 PHB also):

  • Your background
  • Your race
  • Your class

    • In Boz's case, she should have been proficient in persuasion based on her Guild Artisan background so that was added to the final sheet

    I wrote out the bonus that applies to each skill check - you may not need to as it's just "Ability Modifier + (if proficient) Proficiency Bonus" but why not make life even easier

    Your proficiency bonus is added in two other very common circumstances: your chance to hit with a weapon you are proficient with

  • Your chance to hit with a weapon you are proficient with
  • Your chance to hit with a spell that requires a spell attack

    • In both cases, just like with skills, you add your proficiency bonus to your ability modifier to get your total bonus.

    Again, like with skills, you don't have to write this out, but you can see in the middle column of Boz's completed sheet that her attack bonus with daggers is +3 (+2 prof, +1 Dex).

    Below that, her spell attack modifier is +5 (+2 prof, +3 Int modifier). More on spellcasting later.

    I added Boz's passive perception to the sheet. Passive skills - that is, your baseline, not making a particular effort level of skill - for any skill is "10 + skill modifier (see p. 175 PHB)"

    So, for Boz, she is not proficient in perception, and her Wisdom modifier is 0. So that's 10 + 0. Worry if Boz is on watch alone.
