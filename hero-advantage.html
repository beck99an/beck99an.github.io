Hero advantage

Almost everything in comp is derived from the power of numbers advantage.

The team with more players alive in the fight has a significant advantage.

The advantage of the team with superior numbers snowballs as numbers become more uneven; gaming math has it that it's exponential.

Why do people hate "leavers" in comp?

If a player drops in comp and the game becomes a 5v6, the team with 5 will almost NEVER win. It would be a stunning defeat for the 6 player team if this happened.

This power dynamic is also present throughout each fight in a normal game: Wherever a numbers advantage exists for either team.

Comp is about using this basic feature of the game to win the objective.
