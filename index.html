A Brief Note Before Diving In

This is very much a "what I would do if" review. It isn't by any stretch the right way or best way. If you find some of it useful, great.

The whole philosophy could be summed up as follows: what can I do to limit the number of times I need to do math or open the PHB during a session?

So, hopefully you find some of the below helpful, and I present it in the hope that it reduces frustration and increases fun at the table!
