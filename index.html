How to start playing Competitive in Overwatch

This site will teach the foundations of competitive Overwatch and will hopefully keep the pyrrhic defeats to a minimum.

Foundations

These are the concepts that produce the "Contest-Regroup" cycle of a competitive match.

  1. Hero advantage
  2. The objective
  3. A game of yards
  4. Ultimate economy
  5. Not tilting

Contest-Regroup: Tactics and calls

Each part of the Contest-Regroup cycle has common voice calls, related to what the team should be doing in specific circumstances.

  1. Grouping-up "Group up"
  2. On the objective "Point!"
  3. Staggering "Chase", "Stagger"
  4. Not getting staggered "Don't get too aggressive"
  5. Not wasting ults "Don't use ults"
  6. Targets "Rein! Rein!", "Soldier is low", "Break the shield"
  7. Not dying [No specific call]
